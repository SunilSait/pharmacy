Our Story

From a Small Store to
Your City's Trusted Pharmacy

MediCare was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: to make quality healthcare accessible to every family. What started as a single-counter pharmacy has grown into a full-service medical store trusted by over 25,000 customers.

We believe healthcare is not just about dispensing medicines — it's about building relationships, offering guidance, and being available when families need us most. Every product we stock is 100% genuine and sourced from authorized distributors.

Our Journey

Two Decades of
Serving the Community

Key milestones that shaped MediCare into the trusted pharmacy it is today.

2005

Founded in Chennai

Dr. Rajan M. opens MediCare's first single-counter pharmacy near City Hospital with a mission to make genuine medicines accessible to all.

2010

Store Expansion

Expanded to a full medical store, adding health devices, baby care products and our first dedicated consultation desk with licensed pharmacists.

2015

ISO Certification & Home Delivery

Achieved ISO 9001 certification for quality management and launched our city-wide home delivery service reaching 10,000+ households.

2020

Digital Prescription Upload Launched

Introduced our online prescription upload system during the pandemic, serving 5,000+ families safely from home during lockdown periods.

2025

Premium Membership & 25K+ Customers

Launched premium membership plans with priority delivery, free consultations and refill reminders. Now proudly serving over 25,000 families across the city.
