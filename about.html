Our Story

From a Small Store to
Your City's Trusted Pharmacy

MediCare was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: to make quality healthcare accessible to every family. What started as a single-counter pharmacy has grown into a full-service medical store trusted by over 25,000 customers.

We believe healthcare is not just about dispensing medicines — it's about building relationships, offering guidance, and being available when families need us most. Every product we stock is 100% genuine and sourced from authorized distributors.
