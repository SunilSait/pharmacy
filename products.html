FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from MediCare.

Over-the-counter (OTC) medicines like pain relievers, antacids, and cough syrups can be purchased without a prescription. However, prescription medicines such as antibiotics, insulin, and controlled substances require a valid doctor's prescription. You can upload your prescription via our website or WhatsApp.

All products are sourced directly from authorized distributors and pharmaceutical companies. We maintain proper cold-chain storage for vaccines and temperature-sensitive medicines. Every batch is verified with manufacturing dates, expiry dates, and batch numbers before dispatch.

We offer same-day delivery within Chennai city limits for orders placed before 3 PM. Delivery is free for orders above ₹500. For orders below ₹500, a flat delivery charge of ₹40 applies. We also offer in-store pickup at no extra charge. Express delivery within 2 hours is available for urgent medicines at ₹99.

Sealed, unopened products can be returned within 7 days of purchase. Damaged or wrong items are replaced immediately at no cost. Due to safety regulations, opened medicines, refrigerated products, and medical devices cannot be returned unless defective. Contact us within 24 hours for damaged items.

We accept cash on delivery, all major UPI apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and net banking. For in-store purchases, we also accept cash and all card payments. Monthly billing is available for chronic medication customers on request.
