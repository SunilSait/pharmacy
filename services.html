FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our ordering and medical services.

How do I upload my prescription?

You can visit our Contact page or Home page, fill out the prescription enquiry form, click upload to add an image or PDF of your doctor's note, and submit it. Our team will contact you within 30 minutes.

Is the home delivery service safe and sanitary?

Yes, 100%. All medicines are packed in sealed, clinical-grade sanitized bags. Refrigerated products (such as insulin) are shipped in specialized temperature-controlled cold boxes.

Are the medicines genuine?

Absolutely. We source all our pharmaceuticals directly from manufacturer-authorized distributors. We guarantee zero counterfeit risk and maintain full batch traceability.

What happens if a medicine is out of stock?

If a medicine is currently out of stock, our pharmacist will contact you to recommend equivalent certified alternatives or request a special order to source it for you within 24 hours.
